How our digital product design agency works

From idea to shipped product

No two products are the same — so no two projects look the same. Here's how we typically move.

01
Discovery
We get obsessed with your problem before we touch any tool. Market analysis, competitor audit, user research — this is how we avoid building the wrong thing beautifully.
02
UX planning
We map every flow, every edge case, every moment a user might give up. Then we prototype and test before a single final screen is designed.
03
Visual direction
We present two distinct visual directions and A/B test them with real users. You choose what goes forward — backed by data, not gut feeling.
04
UI design & handoff
High-fidelity screens, a complete design system, developer-ready Figma files. Everything your team needs to build — or we build it for you.
05
Development
Frontend, backend, QA — one team, no handoff friction. We own the full stack so nothing gets lost in translation.
06
Launch & support
We don't disappear at launch. Retainer or per-task — we stay in the room as your product grows.

Got a question for Fivecube?

Frequently asked questions

Do you have experience working with early-stage startups?+
Yes — and not just experience. Most of our clients are founders building their first or second product. We've designed MVPs, rebuilt onboarding flows, and shipped full products from zero. We understand that speed and budget discipline matter as much as the design itself.
Which payment options does your digital product design agency work with?+
Staged payments tied to milestones, or monthly retainers for ongoing work. We'll find a structure that works for your budget and project type.
How fast can you develop an MVP?+
Most MVPs take 3–6 weeks depending on scope and complexity. The biggest variable is usually how fast decisions get made on your side — and we'll help you structure that process too.
What if the output doesn't meet my expectations?+
Every project includes two revision rounds per stage. We also run a thorough discovery phase upfront so expectations are aligned before a single screen is designed.
How often do you communicate with the client?+
Weekly or bi-weekly check-ins depending on pace. Plus daily async updates via Slack, WhatsApp, Discord, or Teams — whatever you already use.
Do you charge per hour or have a fixed price list?+
UX audits are fixed price. Design and development projects are typically hourly with a detailed upfront estimate. Fixed-price contracts are available for fully locked scopes.
Do you offer development services in addition to design?+
Yes. Fivecube is a full-cycle agency. UI/UX design, frontend, backend, QA, and deployment — one team, one point of accountability, no messy handoffs between vendors.
Can you support my product post-launch?+
Absolutely. Many of our clients stay on retainer long after launch. We provide ongoing design and development support — on retainer or per-task, depending on what you need.
